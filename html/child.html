Orphan Children

care · education · family · future

Every child deserves a family

Our orphan support program goes beyond shelter. We create loving, stable environments where children can heal, learn, and thrive. Through holistic care, education, and psychosocial support, we build foundations for independent futures.

  • Family-style homes & foster care
  • School fees, uniforms & tutoring
  • Counselling & mentorship
  • Nutrition & health programs

How we support orphans

Love & care

Trained “house parents” live with children, offering emotional support and a sense of belonging.

Quality education

We partner with local schools and provide after‑class learning centres, libraries, and digital skills.

Skills & independence

Vocational training and internship opportunities for teenagers to prepare for self‑sufficiency.

Stories of resilience

Marie, 14 — “After losing my parents, I thought I had to drop out of school. The program gave me a new home and paid my school fees. Now I want to be a nurse.”

In 2025, we supported 230 orphaned children across 3 centres. 98% attended school regularly and 87% improved emotional wellbeing scores.
